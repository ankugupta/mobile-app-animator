About Us

Pelican AR is an Interactive Books App. With Pelican , you can interact with hundreds of printed books. All you need to do is just open the Pelican AR app and point your phone/tablet camera towards a page in a Pelican powered book, and the page will come to life on your mobile device.

Pelican AR works with all kinds of books like colouring books, story books, English, Hindi, Science, Physics, Chemistry, Social Studies and much more.

Steps to use Pelican AR:
  1. Open Pelican AR app
  2. Search for your Book
  3. Press "Get this Book" button
  4. Press "Scan" button
  5. Scan a page in the book
