about Us

Print Mirchi AR is an Interactive Books App. With Print Mirchi, you can interact with hundreds of printed books. All you need to do is just Print Mirchi AR app and point your phone/tablet camera towards a page in a Print Mirchi powered book, and the page will come to life on your mobile device.

Print Mirchi AR works with all kinds of books like colouring books, story books, English, Hindi, Science, Physics, Chemistry, Social Studies and much more.

Steps to use Print Mirchi AR:
  1. Download Print Mirchi AR from Play Store or App store.
  2. Open Print Mirchi AR app
  3. Search for your Book
  4. Press "Get this Book" button
  5. Press "Scan" button
  6. Scan a page in the book
