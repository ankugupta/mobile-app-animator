Harbour Press International

This section is an Interactive Books feature. With Harbour Press International, you can interact with hundreds of printed books. All you need to do is just open the Harbour Press International AR app and point your phone/tablet camera towards a page in a Harbour Press International powered book, and the page will come to life on your mobile device.

Harbour Press International AR works with all kinds of books like colouring books, story books, English, Hindi, Science, Physics, Chemistry, Social Studies and much more.

Steps to use Harbour Press International AR:
  1. Open Harbour Press International AR app
  2. Select a category
  3. Search for your Book
  4. Press "Get this Book" button
  5. Press "Scan" button
  6. Scan a page in the book
