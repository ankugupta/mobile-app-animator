About Us

Dachepalli AR is an Interactive Books App. With Dachepalli , you can interact with hundreds of printed books. All you need to do is just open the Dachepalli AR app and point your phone/tablet camera towards a page in a Dachepalli powered book, and the page will come to life on your mobile device.

Dachepalli AR works with all kinds of books like colouring books, story books, English, Hindi, Science, Physics, Chemistry, Social Studies and much more.

Steps to use Dachepalli AR:
  1. Open Dachepalli AR app
  2. Select a category
  3. Search for your Book
  4. Press "Get this Book" button
  5. Press "Scan" button
  6. Scan a page in the book
