about Us

Berry Garden AR is an Interactive Books App. With Berry Garden, you can interact with hundreds of printed books. All you need to do is just Berry Garden AR app and point your phone/tablet camera towards a page in a Berry Garden powered book, and the page will come to life on your mobile device.

Berry Garden AR works with all kinds of books like colouring books, story books, English, Hindi, Science, Physics, Chemistry, Social Studies and much more.

Steps to use Berry Garden AR:
  1. Download Berry Garden AR from Play Store or App store.
  2. Open Berry Garden AR app
  3. Search for your Book
  4. Press "Get this Book" button
  5. Press "Scan" button
  6. Scan a page in the book
