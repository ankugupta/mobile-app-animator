It is an Interactive Books feature. With Chubby Cheeks, you can interact with hundreds of printed books. All you need to do is just open the Chubby Cheeks AR app and point your phone/tablet camera towards a page in a Chubby Cheeks powered book, and the page will come to life on your mobile device.

Chubby Cheeks AR works with all kinds of books like colouring books, story books, English, Hindi, Science, Physics, Chemistry, Social Studies and much more.

Steps to use Chubby Cheeks AR:
  1. Open Chubby Cheeks AR app
  2. Tap on Chubby Cheeks logo
  3. Select a category
  4. Search for your Book
  5. Press "Get this Book" button
  6. Press "Scan" button
  7. Scan a page in the book
