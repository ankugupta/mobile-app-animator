Berry Garden AR has two sections - Berry Garden and Jugnu Kids.

Berry Garden

This section is an Interactive Books feature. With Berry Garden, you can interact with hundreds of printed books. All you need to do is just open the Berry Garden AR app and point your phone/tablet camera towards a page in a Berry Garden powered book, and the page will come to life on your mobile device.

Berry Garden AR works with all kinds of books like colouring books, story books, English, Hindi, Science, Physics, Chemistry, Social Studies and much more.

Steps to use Berry Garden AR:
  1. Open Berry Garden AR app
  2. Tap on Berry Garden logo
  3. Select a category
  4. Search for your Book
  5. Press "Get this Book" button
  6. Press "Scan" button
  7. Scan a page in the book

Jungu Kids

This section is a video streaming feature. With Jungu Kids, you can enjoy your stories and poems as they come to life on your screen in the form of videos.

Steps to use Jungu Kids:
  1. Open Berry Garden AR app
  2. Tap on Jungu Kids logo
  3. Tap on a video
  4. Tap the play button
