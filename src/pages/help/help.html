What is Print Mirchi

Print Mirchi adds fun and interactivity to exiting printed books using AR (Augmented Reality) technology.

Search

Search and download your book from our library.

Scan

Point the camera at the page you want to read.

Make sure there is optimum lighting and at least a 10 inch distance between the camera and the book.

My Books

"My Books" contains all your downloaded books.

My Pages

"My Pages" contains all your downloaded pages.
