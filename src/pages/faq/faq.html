What is Pelican AR?
Pelican AR is the future of books. It adds fun and interactivity to books. With Pelican AR, users can experience books in multimedia format on their mobile devices.
Is Pelican AR app Free to use or do I need to pay?
Pelican AR Apps is Free to use. But in order to use it effectively, you might(or might not) need to buy the printed book(s) powered by Pelican .
