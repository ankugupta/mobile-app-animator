What is Berry Garden AR?
Berry Garden AR is the future of Books. Berry Garden enables Book Publishers to add fun and interactivity to their books. With Berry Garden AR, Publishers can create different types of Mobile Apps for their Books. These apps are a perfect way to deliver multimedia content to book readers.
How does it work?
Berry Garden AR has a huge collection of interactive book ideas for different Subjects and topics. A Publisher can choose ideas for his book(s), and can create interactive apps for his books.
Is Berry Garden AR app Free to download or do I need to pay?
Berry Garden AR Apps is Free to download. But in order to use it effectively, you might(or might not) need to buy the printed book(s) powered by Berry Garden.
How many Books have used Berry Garden AR till date? And which solution did they use?
More and more books are adopting Berry Garden everyday. So far, 1500+ books have used Berry Garden AR.
